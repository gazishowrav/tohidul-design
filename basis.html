BASIS.org.bd – Corporate Website Redesign

A full redesign and redevelopment of the Bangladesh Association of Software and Information Services (BASIS) website, built using Vue.js to create a modern, scalable, and content-driven platform.

Project Type

Corporate Website Development

Project Overview

BASIS, the national trade body for Bangladesh’s software and IT industry, needed a complete website modernization. The goal was to improve accessibility, provide structured member information, and highlight national IT initiatives while maintaining a strong institutional presence.

The Challenge

The previous website had outdated technology, poor performance, and limited content management flexibility. BASIS required a modern, responsive, and easily maintainable platform that could serve members, media, and stakeholders efficiently.

The Solution

I developed the front-end architecture using Vue.js, integrating dynamic routing and modular components to support scalability. The design system was built around a clean layout with consistent branding, accessibility compliance, and optimized performance for a wide audience.

Key Highlights

  • Vue.js SPA Architecture: Developed a modular single-page application for smoother navigation and performance.
  • Dynamic Member Directory: Implemented a searchable and filterable database of BASIS members and IT firms.
  • Responsive Redesign: Fully mobile-friendly layout optimized for devices of all sizes.
  • Content Management Integration: Connected with backend APIs for real-time news, events, and membership data.
  • Accessibility & Localization: Supported bilingual content (English & Bangla) for inclusivity.

Outcome

The updated BASIS website successfully established a modern digital presence for Bangladesh’s IT industry. It improved content discoverability, member visibility, and user engagement — aligning BASIS’s online experience with its role as a leading industry platform.
