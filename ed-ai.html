The Challenge

The international university application process is notoriously complex, fragmented, and overwhelming for students. They struggle with finding the right-fit courses from thousands of options, managing disparate application documents, and preparing for high-stakes visa or university interviews.

Our Solution: An Intelligent, Centralized Assistant

We designed EdAI, a centralized, AI-driven platform to act as a student's personal admissions assistant. The system simplifies the entire journey by providing personalized "AI Match Scores" for courses, a step-by-step application tracker, and a unique AI-powered interview practice module with a "Confidence Score" to build user confidence.

Key Features & Outcomes

  • Personalized Onboarding: A 7-step wizard to customize the user's dashboard and provide tailored recommendations.
  • "AI Match Score": A gamified, data-driven feature to help students quickly assess their fit for a specific course.
  • AI Interview Practice: A module that allows users to practice university, visa, or language interviews and receive a "Confidence Score" feedback.
  • Unified Dashboard: A central command center to track application progress, pending documents, and scheduled counseling sessions.
